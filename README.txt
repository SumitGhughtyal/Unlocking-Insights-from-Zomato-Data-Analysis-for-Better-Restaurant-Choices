## Overview
This project dives deep into the **Zomato restaurant dataset** to extract meaningful insights about restaurant ratings, cost, online ordering, and table booking availability. The goal is to understand customer preferences and analyze the influence of services on restaurant popularity.

## Project Highlights
- ‚≠ê **Top-rated Restaurants**: Analyzing the highest-rated restaurants based on customer votes.
- üí∞ **Cost Analysis**: Comparing the approximate cost for two people at various establishments.
- üì≤ **Online Ordering**: Investigating how the availability of online ordering affects restaurant performance.
- ü™ë **Table Booking**: Understanding the role of table booking services in customer satisfaction.

## Dataset
The dataset contains information about:
- Restaurant name, ratings, votes.
- Cost for two people.
- Availability of online ordering and table booking.
- Restaurant type (e.g., Buffet, Delivery).

## Libraries and Tools
- **Pandas & NumPy**: Data handling and manipulation.
- **Matplotlib & Seaborn**: Data visualization.
- **Jupyter Notebook**: For executing the analysis and generating insights.

## Visualizations
- Bar charts and pie charts to compare ratings, costs, and services.
- Heatmaps to show correlations between different factors like ratings, votes, and costs.
